# Catalyst: Your Ultimate Fitness & Health Companion

Catalyst is a premium, all-in-one fitness and health tracking application designed to help you stay disciplined, hydrated, and focused on your training goals. Built with a sleek, modern aesthetic and intuitive interactions, Catalyst turns your health journey into a streamlined, data-driven experience.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Smart Training Dashboard
Your daily command center. Catalyst automatically identifies your workout for the day based on your training split.
- **Dynamic Daily Focus:** Instantly see what's on the agenda (e.g., Pull Day, Push Day, or Active Recovery).
- **Interactive Exercise Cards:** Log weights, reps, and sets in real-time. Cards update visually as you complete each exercise.
- **Cardio & Strength Support:** Tailored inputs for both weightlifting (kg) and cardio (minutes/intensity).
- **Tempo Reminders:** Built-in guidance to ensure you maintain the right pace for maximum hypertrophy or endurance.

### 2. Advanced Hydration Tracker
Water tracking redefined. Catalyst features a unique **Hydration Clock** to ensure you stay hydrated throughout the entire day, not just after your workout.
- **Hydration Clock:** A visual representation of your water intake across a 24-hour cycle. Distinguishes between AM and PM intake.
- **Interactive Tank UI:** Watch your progress fill up a glassmorphic tank in real-time as you approach your daily goal.
- **Quick Logging:** Add or remove water intake with a single tap.
- **Goal Achievement:** Visual celebrations when you hit your hydration targets.

### 3. Weekly Overview & Smart Scheduling
Take control of your entire week. Catalyst provides a birds-eye view of your 7-day training split.
- **Shiftable Schedules:** Feeling sore? Life got in the way? Easily "shift" your schedule to make any training day "Today's Workout" with one tap.
- **Split Visibility:** See every exercise, set, and rep requirement for the entire week at a glance.
- **Recovery Strategy:** Clearly identified recovery days with specific instructions to ensure you don't overtrain.

### 4. Motivational Notifications
Stay on track with smart, context-aware prompts.
- **Morning Wake-Up:** Get a boost to start your day and a preview of your training focus.
- **Evening Reflection:** Review your progress and confirm your gym session completion.
- **Hydration Prompts:** Gentle nudges to keep your water intake consistent.

### 5. Premium Design Aesthetic
Catalyst isn't just functional—it's beautiful.
- **Sleek Dark Mode:** Optimized for low-light environments and a premium feel.
- **Glassmorphic Elements:** Modern UI components with subtle transparency and blur effects.
- **Vibrant Visual Feedback:** Smooth animations and color transitions that respond to your progress.
