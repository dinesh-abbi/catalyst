Here is a highly structured, day-wise plan for exactly 4 weeks (28 days). It is formatted with clear, repetitive keys so you can easily parse it into a JSON object for your app. 

This plan accounts for your family’s sattvic (no onion, no garlic) vegetarian diet, while allocating specific days for you to prepare a quick side of eggs or chicken in a separate pan to meet your fat loss and muscle maintenance targets. 

I have also integrated **SuperYou** protein. SuperYou's Fermented Yeast Protein is an excellent choice for your goals; it has a PDCAAS score of 1.0 (meaning it absorbs as effectively as whey) but is vegan, dairy-free, and gut-friendly, preventing the bloating often associated with whey. It also includes probiotics to support digestion. A 1kg tub of flavors like Chocolate or Cold Coffee is currently priced around ₹2,249.
